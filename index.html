About Us

We Are Dev Priya Product....

Dev Priya Product Limited, is a manufacturing unit of Kraft Liner and Coated Board by recycling of waste paper. Dev Priya Product Ltd. total installed capacity is around 2,44,000 MT of Packaging Board & Kraft Liner. Dev Priya Product Ltd. has a huge distribution network in India and also has a very good Potential in overseas market like Srilanka / Bangladesh / Nepal / Middle East and African continent.
